The Biography of Nicholas Metzger

I was born in Providence, Rhode Island to my loving parents, Amanda and Jonathan. I grew up in Norwell, Massachusetts. I have two brothers, Charlie, 20, and Ben, 14. I have a dog named Leo and a cat named Artemis and 5 chickens. I am in love with food. Some of my favorite restaurants are Red Bones, Border Cafe, and Shake Shack.
